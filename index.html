Greenspoon Food Safety Portfolio

🔍 Overview

Since joining Greenspoon, I have led major improvements in warehouse hygiene, supplier handling, and food safety compliance. My work focuses on aligning operations with international food safety standards (ISO 22000 & HACCP), while creating a culture of accountability and proactive risk management.

🧠 Key Initiatives & Achievements

  • ✅ Pallet Replacement: Eliminated contamination risks by replacing wooden pallets with food-grade plastic alternatives.
  • 📋 Policy Leadership: Drafted and rolled out PPE and Food Safety Policies, ensuring clear, enforceable standards.
  • 🧼 Warehouse Sanitation: Instituted daily and scheduled cleaning, dramatically improving hygiene.
  • 🧊 Cooler Box System: Implemented a continuous one-time-use cooler box washing system to prevent microbial buildup.
  • 📦 Receiving Area Zoning: Organized receiving into 4 distinct areas:
    • Fruits & vegetables
    • Chilled & frozen items
    • Dry goods
    • Non-food items
  • 🚛 Supplier Management: Developed a supplier tracking system based on arrival time, service speed, and hygiene performance.
  • 🧪 HACCP Implementation: Leading the compilation of Greenspoon’s HACCP plan alongside food safety champions.
  • 👥 Staff Training: Ongoing food safety training sessions for all warehouse and receiving staff to strengthen internal capacity.

📈 Outcome Highlights

  • Marked improvement in hygiene across warehouse operations
  • Streamlined supplier flow and minimized cross-contamination risks
  • Greater readiness for third-party audits and certifications
